About me

User Experience design and development is the perfect combination for someone who is artistic, but enjoys maths. I didn’t know what UX design was until well into my Graphic Design degree, but by the time I graduated I quickly found myself graduating towards tech companies and startups. After working at app companies for a few years, I started freelancing in order to explore a wider range of projects.

While I love the creativity involved in illustrating, writing and UI design, much of my focus goes towards thinking about systems, designing logical flows and problem solving. I enjoy seeing the entire process of product design through to the end, particularly when it means getting involved with building once the design phase is over.
